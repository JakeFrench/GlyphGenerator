8x8 Glyph Generator

emailjakefrench@googlemail.com

A PyQt app for drawing out 8x8 Glyph projects – The resultant Hex array for each line may be used to hard-encode Glyphs:

Data might well be stored on the microcontroller Flash (program memory) like this:

uint8_t invader[8] PROGMEM = {0x18, 0x3c, 0x7e, 0xdb, 0xff, 0x24, 0x5a, 0xa5};
